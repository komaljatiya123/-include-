About #Include

The story behind SGSITS IT Department's premier technical club

Our History

Started in November 2017 by passionate IT students with a vision.

#Include was founded by IT 4th year students who felt the need to have a practical approach towards the technical aspects of engineering. The club was created to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications.

Since its inception, the club has become a city-wide sensation and is successfully hosting various contests and sessions. This technical community includes around 30 members from three batches working together to create a vibrant learning environment.

Our Mission

The motive of the club is to create a student learning community, which together will coordinate and help each other to gain technical knowledge.

We aim to provide learning opportunities by collaborating with technical companies and institutions. Our activities are designed to enhance both technical skills and soft skills necessary for future endeavors.

We believe in learning by doing, and encourage all our members to take up projects and participate in hackathons and coding competitions.

Our Vision

We have a vision that every student passing out of SGSITS and belonging to the community will be technically skilled as well as have gained soft skills necessary for every individual for future endeavours.

We strive to create an environment where students can experiment, innovate, and grow together. Through our various events and initiatives, we aim to nurture talent and create opportunities for students to showcase their skills.

Key Achievements

HackIndore

Successfully organized multiple editions of HackIndore, a 24-hour hackathon that attracts participants from colleges across the region.

Industry Collaborations

Established partnerships with leading tech companies and the Mozilla technical community for workshops and events.

Community Building

Created a thriving technical community with active participation in tech events, coding contests, and project development.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Encouraging creative thinking and innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Collaboration

Working together to achieve common goals and learn from each other.

Continuous Learning

Fostering a culture of lifelong learning and skill development.

Excellence

Striving for excellence in everything we do, from contests to projects.
